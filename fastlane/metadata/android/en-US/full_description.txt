<b>Glowmate</b> — In a world of noise, Glowmate is your signal. Secure, open, and always yours.

Glowmate is a completely free and open source app for universal, distributed peer-to-peer communication that respects the freedom and privacy of its users. Developed by <b>Quanta Inc.</b>, a one of a kind company focusing on the liberation of humanity from the Matrix; providing free and open source software, products and services with ZERO profit motive.

<b>Why Glowmate?</b>
Glowmate is the simplest and easiest way to connect with people with instant messaging, audio, and video calls over the <b>Internet</b> and LAN/WAN <b>intranets</b> — with no central servers, no tracking, and no surveillance.

<b>Key Features:</b>
🔒 End-to-end encrypted messaging and calls
🌐 Fully decentralized — no central servers, ever
📞 Audio and video calls directly between users
📁 File sharing and transfers
👥 One-to-one and group conversations
📍 Location sharing
🖥️ Screen sharing
🎙️ Audio and video messages
📺 Android TV support
🔌 SIP client support
🆓 100% free, open source, and ad-free

<b>Privacy First:</b>
Glowmate calls are <b>directly between users</b> — no servers handle your calls. The <b>distributed</b> nature of Glowmate means your conversations are only between participants. Always.

<b>Available on:</b>
GNU/Linux, Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, Android TV
