I-Jami, iphakheji ye-GNU, isofthiwe yokuxhumana kwamazwe ngamazwe nokusatshalaliswa kontanga ehlonipha inkululeko nobumfihlo babasebenzisi bayo.

I-Jami iyindlela elula nelula kakhulu yokuxhumana nabantu (namadivayisi) ngemiyalezo esheshayo, amakholi omsindo nevidiyo nge-inthanethi kanye nama-intranethi e-LAN/WAN.

I-Jami iyinkundla yamahhala/ekhululekile, ebethelwe ngasemaphethelweni, nenkundla yokuxhumana yangasese.

I-Jami - ebikade yaziwa ngokuthi yiNdandatho - futhi ingenye yemithombo evulekile (ku-Facebook Messenger, Signal, Skype, Teams, Telegraph, TikTok, Viber, WhatsApp, Zoom) ebeka phambili ubumfihlo babasebenzisi bayo.

I-Jami inomklamo obukeka njengochwepheshe futhi itholakala kumapulatifomu amaningi. Ngokungafani nezinye izindlela, izingcingo ezisebenzisa i-Jami ziphakathi kwabasebenzisi ngokuqondile njengoba ingasebenzisi amaseva ukusingatha izingcingo.

Lokhu kunikeza ubumfihlo obukhulu kakhulu njengoba imvelo esabalalisiwe ye-Jami isho ukuthi amakholi akho aphakathi kwabahlanganyeli kuphela.

Izingxoxo zomuntu nomuntu kanye neqembu no-Jami zithuthukiswa ngokuthi: umlayezo osheshayo; ukushaya komsindo nevidiyo; ukurekhoda nokuthumela imiyalezo yomsindo nevidiyo; ukudluliselwa kwamafayela; ukwabelana kwesikrini; futhi, ukwabelana ngendawo.

I-Jami ingaphinda isebenze njengeklayenti le-SIP.

I-Jami inezandiso eziningi ezitholakalayo: Isihlungi Somsindo; Impendulo ezenzakalelayo; Isikrini Esiluhlaza; I-Watermark; kanye ne-Whisper Transcript.

I-Jami ingafakwa kalula ezinhlanganweni ezine-“Jami Account Management Server” (JAMS), evumela abasebenzisi ukuthi baxhumane nemininingwane yabo yenkampani noma badale ama-akhawunti endawo. I-JAMS ikuvumela ukuthi uphathe owakho umphakathi we-Jami ngenkathi usebenzisa i-architecture yenethiwekhi esabalalisiwe ye-Jami.

I-Jami iyatholakala ku-GNU/Linux, Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, ne-Android TV, okwenza i-Jami ibe uhlaka lokuxhumana olusebenzisanayo nolwezingxenyekazi ezihlukene.

Phatha ama-akhawunti amaningi e-SIP, ama-akhawunti e-Jami nama-akhawunti e-JAMS ngeklayenti le-Jami efakwe kudivayisi eyodwa noma eziningi.

I-Jami imahhala, ayinamkhawulo, iyimfihlo, imahhala ukukhangisa, iyahambisana, iyashesha, izimele, futhi ingaziwa.
