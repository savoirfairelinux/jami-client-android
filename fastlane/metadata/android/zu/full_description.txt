I-<b>Jami</b>, iphakheji ye-GNU, isofthiwe yokuxhumana kontanga kuya kontanga yomhlaba wonke nesabalalisiwe ehlonipha inkululeko nobumfihlo babasebenzisi bayo.

I-Jami iyindlela elula nelula kakhulu yokuxhumana nabantu (namadivayisi) ngemiyalezo esheshayo, umsindo, namakholi wevidiyo <b>nge-inthanethi</b> kanye ne-LAN/WAN <b>intranet</b>.

I-Jami iyinhlangano yamahhala/ekhululekile, ebethelwe ngasemaphethelweni, futhi <b>inkundla yokuxhumana</b> eyimfihlo.

I-Jami <b>iyisofthiwe yomthombo ovulekile</b> <b>ebeka kuqala ubumfihlo bomsebenzisi</b>.

I-Jami inomklamo obukeka njengochwepheshe futhi itholakala kumapulatifomu amaningi. Ngokungafani nezinye, izingcingo ze-Jami <b>ziphakathi kwabasebenzisi ngokuqondile</b>, njengoba ingasebenzisi amaseva ukubamba amakholi.

Lokhu kunikeza ubumfihlo obukhulu kakhulu, njengoba <b>imvelo</b> esabalalisiwe ye-Jami isho ukuthi amakholi akho aphakathi kwabahlanganyeli kuphela.

Izingxoxo zomuntu oyedwa kanye <b>neqembu</b> no-Jami zithuthukiswa ngokuthumela umlayezo osheshayo, ukushaya kwevidiyo nomsindo, ukurekhoda nokuthumela imilayezo yomsindo nevidiyo, ukudluliselwa kwamafayela, ukwabelana kwesikrini, nokwabelana ngendawo.

I-Jami ingaphinda isebenze <b>njengeklayenti le-SIP</b>.

<b>Izandiso ze-Jami</b> eziningi ziyatholakala: Isihlungi Somsindo, Ukuphendula Okuzenzakalelayo, Isikrini Esiluhlaza, Isegimenti, I-Watermark, ne-Whisper Transcript.

I-Jami ingatshalwa kalula ezinhlanganweni <b>ezine-JAMS (Iseva Yokuphathwa Kwe-akhawunti Ye-Jami)</b>, evumela abasebenzisi ukuthi baxhumane nemininingwane yabo yebhizinisi noma badale ama-akhawunti endawo. I-JAMS ikuvumela ukuthi <b>uphathe</b> umphakathi wakho we-Jami ngenkathi usebenzisa i-architecture yenethiwekhi esabalalisiwe ye-Jami.

I-Jami <b>iyatholakala ku-</b> GNU/Linux, Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, Android TV, kanye neziphequluli zewebhu, okwenza i-Jami ibe uhlaka lokuxhumana olusebenzisanayo nolwezingxenyekazi ezihlukene.

<b>Phatha ama-akhawunti amaningi</b> e-SIP, ama-akhawunti e-Jami, nama-akhawunti e-JAMS ngeklayenti le-Jami efakwe kudivayisi eyodwa noma eziningi.

I-Jami imahhala, ayinamkhawulo, iyimfihlo, ayikhangisi, iyahambisana, iyashesha, izimele, futhi ingaziwa.
