## About Glowmate

Glowmate is a completely free and open source secure messaging and communication app. Built on top of a fully decentralized, peer-to-peer architecture — no servers, no tracking, no surveillance.

Developed by **Quanta Inc.**, a one of a kind company focusing on the liberation of humanity from the Matrix; providing free and open source software, products and services with ZERO profit motive.

🌐 Visit [quantainc.com](https://quantainc.com) to learn more.

## Features

- 🔒 End-to-end encrypted messaging & calls
- 🌐 Fully decentralized — no central servers
- 📞 Audio & video calls
- 📁 File sharing
- 👥 Group conversations
- 🆓 100% free and open source (GPL-3.0)
